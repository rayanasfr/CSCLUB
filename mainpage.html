    Fadi CS Club is an online community passionate about Computer Science. Our mission is to provide our members with tips, resources, and support to discover, learn, and improve their coding knowledge while mastering tough concepts and writing better code. Through learning and teaching from each other, we strive to foster a community of individuals dedicated to advancing their coding skills and understanding in the world of technology.
